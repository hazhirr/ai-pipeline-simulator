AI Pipeline Model — Interactive Simulator by Hazhir Rahmandad (hazhir@mit.edu)

A live, browser-based version of the AI Pipeline system-dynamics model. The industry starts in equilibrium; AI capability begins growing at month 10 and progressively automates a feasible fraction of ordinary work (and, with delay, expert work). Adjust the parameters on the left and watch how the industry's output, employment, wages, and profits evolve over 500 months.
